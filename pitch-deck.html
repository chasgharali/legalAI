The Problem

A 5,000-page case file.
50 paralegal hours.
One missed date and the case dies.

Every UK clinical-negligence and PI claim begins the same way: a fee-earner ploughs through hospital records, GP notes, A&E sheets and consultant letters to assemble a date-ordered medical chronology and a barrister-ready bundle. It is repetitive, expensive, error-prone, and the single biggest time sink in the practice area.

The Solution

Upload the file. Get a verifiable chronology, a court-ready bundle, and a chat that cites every line.

01 / CHRONOLOGY

Auto-extracted, source-anchored

Every clinical event: date, provider, presenting complaint, diagnosis, treatment, relevance flag. Every entry cites a verbatim quote and a real page number. Treatment gaps and inconsistencies surfaced automatically.

02 / BUNDLE

Barrister-ready PDF in one click

Cover sheet, table of contents, full chronology, document index, watermark, page numbers, A4. Generated to the format counsel actually expects, not a print-to-PDF browser dialog.

03 / CHAT

Ask anything about the case

Vector-retrieved entries, GPT-4o reasoning, every answer cited back to a specific entry and source page. “Was there a pre-existing back condition?” — answered with evidence in seconds.

How It Works

Built around the workflow, not bolted onto it.

01 — INGEST
OCR + page tracking

Drag & drop GP notes, hospital records, consultant letters. AWS Textract OCR for scanned pages; per-page text preserved with real page numbers.

02 — EXTRACT
Structured chronology

GPT-4o produces typed JSON: 14 event types, 4 relevance flags, treatment-gap detection, inconsistency flags. Background queue, retries on failure.

03 — VERIFY
Fee-earner workflow

Every entry has a Verified checkbox. Bundles watermarked “AI-Generated — Review Required” until signed off. SRA-defensible.

04 — DELIVER
Bundle + chat

One-click PDF for counsel. Vector-retrieved chat with [entry-id] citations. Audit trail of every query and download.
